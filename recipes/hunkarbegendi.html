Hünkar Beğendi (Sultan’s Delight)

Indegredients
  • 500g lamb, cubed
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 tomatoes, peeled & chopped
  • 1 green pepper, sliced
  • Salt & pepper to taste
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 4 eggplants
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 2 tbsp flour
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup grated kaşar cheese or mozzarella

Method

  1. Cook the Lamb: In a pot, heat olive oil, sauté onions until soft. Add garlic, lamb, salt, pepper, paprika, and cumin. Cook until browned. Add tomatoes and green pepper. Simmer until lamb is tender.
  2. Prepare the Eggplant Purée: Roast the eggplants over an open flame until charred. Peel, mash, and set aside.
  3. Make the Béchamel Sauce: In a pan, melt butter, add flour, and stir for 2-3 minutes. Gradually whisk in milk until thickened. Add mashed eggplant and cheese. Stir until smooth.
  4. Serve: Plate the eggplant purée, top with lamb mixture, and serve hot.
